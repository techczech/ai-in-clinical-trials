# The AI Toolkit

We utilized a suite of specialized AI tools to both conduct the research and build this presentation.

- **Consensus**: AI-powered search engine for research papers. [consensus.app](https://consensus.app)
- **Elicit**: Research assistant for literature review and reports. [elicit.com](https://elicit.com)
- **ChatGPT (GPT-5.2)**: Large language model for drafting, analysis, and coding. [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com)
- **NotebookLM**: Source-grounded AI for document synthesis. [notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com)
- **Antigravity**: Agentic AI coding assistant and data analyst. [github.com/google-deepmind/antigravity](https://github.com/google-deepmind/antigravity)
