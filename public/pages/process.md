# Behind the Science

The process of conducting this research and building this website was entirely AI-driven.

## Methodology

1. **Data Gathering**: Querying Consensus (research Search), Elicit (Literature Review), and ChatGPT Deep Research (Case Studies) for frontier insights.
2. **Synthesis**: Using NotebookLM to ground findings in Oxford-specific constraints, SOPs, and clinical roles.
3. **Exhibit Build**: Architecting this presentation using Antigravity with Gemini 3 Flash to create a searchable, browsable research portal.
